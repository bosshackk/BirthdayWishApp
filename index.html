Wishes

      







“On your birthday
we wish for you that whatever you want most in life
it comes to you just the way you imagined it or better.
Happy birthday!”

Poem

    Forget about the past,
        you can’t change it.
    Forget about the future,
        you can’t predict it.
      And forget about the present,
        I didn’t get you one. 
        
    Happy birthday!
